# 2020 YYC Investment Summit Document

# **Startup TNT Investment Summit XI**

This document synthesizes the key outcomes, investment data, and company profiles from the Startup TNT Investment Summit XI held in Calgary. The document covers both the Top 20 Pitch Night (April 3, 2025\) and the Finale Pitch Night (May 1, 2025), detailing the growth of the regional innovation ecosystem and the specific startups receiving investment.

## **Executive Summary**

Startup TNT’s Investment Summit XI marks a significant milestone in the Western Canadian venture capital landscape, coinciding with the organization's five-year anniversary. Since its inception in 2019, Startup TNT has facilitated over $17.4 million in investments across 110 companies, supported by a network of nearly 490 angel investors.

The Calgary chapter has been particularly impactful, activating 157 local angel investors and deploying $7.4 million into 40 companies. Summit XI concluded with **ResVR** being named the primary winner, while three additional side deals were secured for **Pharma Pilot**, **Village Wellth**, and **Tarkka Manufacturing Solutions**. The summit highlighted a diverse array of sectors, including medical devices, AI-driven real estate, quantum computing, and clean technology.

## **Organizational Overview: The Startup TNT Model**

Startup TNT (originally "Thursday Night Tradition") began as a grassroots initiative in Edmonton to address the shortage of early-stage capital. It has since expanded across Western Canada, including Calgary, Saskatchewan, Winnipeg, and British Columbia.

### **Investment Process**

* **Syndicated Angel Investing:** Investors pre-commit a minimum of $5,000 each before knowing the eventual recipient. For Summit XI, a pool of approximately 34-42 investors participated.  
* **Team-Based Due Diligence:** The process is not a simple pitch competition but a six-week collaborative due diligence period. Investors review data rooms, interview founders, examine financials, and build relationships.  
* **Active Investing:** While the summit crowns a single winner for the main syndicate, the model frequently results in side deals where individual investors or sub-groups fund other finalists.  
* **Market Position:** Startup TNT is currently ranked as the most active pre-seed investor in Canada and the second-highest private non-government agency for seed-stage deals.

### **Regional Impact (Calgary)**

The Calgary ecosystem demonstrates high levels of diversity and engagement:

* **Total Investment:** $7.4 million (43% of Startup TNT's total capital).  
* **Founder Diversity:** 53% of companies are female-founded and 25% are BIPOC founded.  
* **Institutional Support:** Strong integration with **Platform Calgary**, the **University of Calgary** (Hunter Hub), and **Alberta Innovates**.

## **Summit XI Finale: Finalist Profiles and Investment Analysis**

Five companies reached the finale after a rigorous screening process. All five were successfully funded or were actively closing rounds at the time of the event.

### **1\. ResVR (Summit Winner)**

* **Core Technology:** 3D interactive, web-based subscription software for homebuilders.  
* **Value Proposition:** Enables home buyers to visualize and customize upgrades in real-time, delivering a measurable 10x ROI for builders by increasing upgrade spend by approximately 30%.  
* **Performance Metrics:** $4 million in annual recurring revenue (ARR) pipeline, 0% churn rate.  
* **Market Strategy:** Expanding into the U.S. market in July 2025, launching an affiliate program for furniture and insurance.  
* **Funding:** Raised 70% of a $2 million round as of May 1, 2025\.

### **2\. Tarkka Manufacturing Solutions (Side Deal)**

* **Core Technology:** Patent pending custom contoured seating for wheelchairs using additive manufacturing and composite materials.  
* **Value Proposition:** Prevents pressure wounds and postural decline, which are leading causes of mortality for wheelchair users.  
* **Market Potential:** $1.3 billion market, target revenue of $28 million in three years.  
* **Business Model:** 80%+ margins through established dealer networks.  
* **Funding:** Raising $1.5 million USD to accelerate commercialization.

### **3\. Pharma Pilot (Side Deal)**

* **Core Technology:** AI-driven pharmacy phone system for inbound and outbound communication.  
* **Value Proposition:** Automates 90% of routine pharmacy calls, freeing pharmacists from 1-3 hours of daily phone interruptions.  
* **Strategic Partnerships:** Integration in progress with **McKesson** (a Fortune 9 company), providing access to 1,100 pharmacies and a U.S. entry point.  
* **Funding:** Raising $300,000; already secured two side deals during the summit.

### **4\. Village Wellth (Side Deal)**

* **Core Technology:** A two sided marketplace and deal management platform for entrepreneurship through acquisition.  
* **Value Proposition:** Addresses the silver tsunami of retiring business owners ($16 trillion in assets transferring by 2032\) by helping buyers find, finance, and close deals.  
* **Performance Metrics:** 15,000+ users; $300,000 ARR; profitable in 2024\.  
* **Growth:** Pathway to $100 million valuation through U.S. expansion and integration with Business Broker CRMs.

### **5\. QTI (Quantized Technologies Inc.)**

* **Core Technology:** Developing the world’s first commercially viable quantum repeater to enable a quantum internet.  
* **Scientific Breakthroughs:** High-bandwidth Quantum Random Access Memory (QRAM) and robust entanglement distribution in noisy environments.  
* **Market Validation:** Early engagement with **AWS**, **Telus**, **Nvidia**, and **Accenture**.  
* **Funding:** Raising a seed round, 65% filled as of the finale.
